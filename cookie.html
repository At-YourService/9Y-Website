2. Cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data that a website asks your browser to store on your device. The cookie allows that website to "remember" your actions or preferences over time.

Duration Types

  • Permanent cookies: Recognize your browser for repeat visits.
  • Session cookies: Recognize your browser during a single visit; deleted at session end.

Origin Types

  • First party cookies: Set by 9Yards directly.
  • Third party cookies: Set by external services (e.g., social media plugins).

Usage Categories

  • Strictly necessary: Essential for website operation and security.
  • Performance & Functional: Monitor usage to optimize content and remember settings.

Managing and blocking cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.
