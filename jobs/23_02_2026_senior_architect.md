# Senior Solutions Architect

![Architecture](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1454165833767-027ffea70288?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=2426)

Are you a visionary architect who thrives on bridging the gap between digital strategy and organizational reality? Join 9Yards as a Senior Solutions Architect.

## Role Overview

As a Senior Solutions Architect, you will lead the design and implementation of complex digital transformation initiatives. You'll work closely with stakeholders to ensure that architectural designs are not only state-of-the-art but also pragmatic and financially viable.

### Responsibilities:

- Lead architectural assessments for large-scale digital transitions.
- Co-design organizational structures that support agile delivery.
- Mentor junior architects and engineers in pragmatic implementation.
- Drive the evaluation of new technologies and their business impact.

### Qualifications:

- 10+ years of experience in enterprise architecture or systems design.
- Proven track record of delivering complex digital transformations.
- Strong communication skills, with the ability to influence C-level stakeholders.
- Deep understanding of modern cloud architectures and data ecosystems.

We offer a high-impact environment where your designs directly shape the future of our clients' organizations.
